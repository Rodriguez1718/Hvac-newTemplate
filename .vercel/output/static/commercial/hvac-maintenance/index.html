Commercial systems should be serviced at least twice a year — once before the cooling season and once before the heating season. High-use environments like restaurants, medical facilities, and data centers may require quarterly maintenance.
Our comprehensive service includes inspecting and cleaning coils, checking refrigerant levels, testing controls and safety devices, lubricating moving parts, inspecting ductwork, checking electrical connections, and calibrating thermostats. We provide a detailed report after each visit.
Regular maintenance can reduce energy costs by 15-25% and extend equipment life by 5-10 years. It also prevents costly emergency breakdowns that can disrupt your business operations and result in lost revenue.
